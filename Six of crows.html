Six of Crows

The Six of Crows is a series of fantasy novels written by American author Leigh Bardugo. This the second series in the Grishaverse. The first book in the series is called "Six of Crows". In this world there are people with the power to influence certain aspects of nature. These people are called "Grisha". The Grisha fall into 3 orders. The Corporalki, The Etherialki and The Materialki. The Corporalki is also known as the Order of the Living and the Dead and their are two types of Grisha in this order: Healers and Heartrenders. Healers specialize in healig wounds and injuries. Heartrenders specialize in combat and can control bloodflow an the heart. They can put people in a coma or just burst your heart with a flick of their fingers and they are generally the highest valued soldiers. The Etherialki is also known as the Order of Summoners can manipulate the nature around them. There are three types of Etherialki: Squallers, Inferni and Tidemakers. Squallers control wind, Inferni control fire and Tidemakers control water. The Materialki can control the physical structure of things. They dont usually fight and are generally the lowest valued soldiers. There are two types of Materialki: Fabrikators and Alkemists. Fabricators deal with solid objects and Alkemists deal with liquids. Materialki are often used to create weapons and high quality materials like glass and steel. In this world there are 5 nations. Their names are Ravka, Fjerda, Kerch, Shu Han and Novyi Zem. Every country is very different in their culture, relgion and views.

The second series takes place in Kerch. Kerch is an island nation and the richest country by far since they have a monopoly on all of trading. In Kerch they worship a god called Ghezen who is the god of trade and commerce. In Kerch Grisha are used as slaves. Although Kerch may seem like a bustling trading city, there is a large crimincal underworld ruled by countless gangs. This place is known as the Barrel and is an expansive area of Kerch past the Pleasure district. The gangs fight over money and property. Most of the pleasure district is owned by the gangs of the Barrel. This duology follows master theif Kaz Brekker also known as Dirtyhands. He is the liuteneant of a gang called "The Dregs". This duology follows the story of Kaz Brekker and his crew as they try break into and steal a hostage kept in the most secure place in Fjerda known as the "Ice Court" to retrieve the secrets of a drug called jurda parem.
